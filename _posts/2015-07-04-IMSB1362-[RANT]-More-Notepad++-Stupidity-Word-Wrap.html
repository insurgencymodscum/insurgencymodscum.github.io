Notepad++ Shortcut Mapper.
No default shortcut for "Word Wrap". No search in "Shortcut Mapper". WTF!

Default shortcuts for "Zoom In" and "Zoom Out" are using numpad. To unfuck this, one has to rebind Ctrl+NumPad+ and Ctrl+Numpad- to more reasonable Ctrl+= and Ctrl+-.

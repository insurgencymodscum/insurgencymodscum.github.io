Simple. Security Forces get night vision equipment.

Insurgents get flashlights. Some insurgents (those with highest scores) get flashlights with shitty batteries that they have to change.  Insurgents with low scores get no batteries (simulating they just forget them at home) and have to check the map and return to the spawn point and pick them up.

This might turn out to be too one-sided though, so this is still work in progress.

P.S. This will promote communication, so this idea definitely has potential.

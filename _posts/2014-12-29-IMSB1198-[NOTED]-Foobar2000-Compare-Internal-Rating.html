--- layout: post title: '[NOTED] Foobar2000: Compare Internal Rating (Playback Statistics) to an Actual Rating' date: '2014-12-29T23:07:00.000Z' author: insurgencymodscum2 tags: - Insurgency Mod Scum - OT-Noted - OT-PROTIP modified_time: '2015-01-01T23:08:10.198Z' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7256856399250838035.post-2885128639057612348 blogger_orig_url: http://insurgencymod.blogspot.com/2014/12/noted-foobar2000-compare-internal.html --- This will show all the files if there's a difference between "%rating%" (an internal rating held in a database by the "Playback Statistics" component) and "$meta(rating)" (an actual rating in the file metadata). Useful if you use two or more music managers (e.g. MusicBee and Foobar2000).

"$strcmp($num($meta(rating),0),$num(%rating%,0))" EQUAL 0

What have we achieved over the last 5 years?
Over the past five years we have seen our organisation change direction and go from strength to strength achieving some impressive results whilst continually striving for bigger cup and event wins.
We have seen our organisation represented over the years at various Lan events ranging from just one team to over 25 members attending under our name.
Whilst previously we supported the lower end of the scene helping new and upcoming players to make their entrance into the Counter-Strike Source scene we have now moved into the higher end of the scene in both Css and CoD4.
Our current UK Css team are ranked 1st in the ESL Amateur Series and are looking strong to gain promotion into the EAS Div1 when the qualifications close. We are also competing the the ClanBase Eurocup Invite Cup where we are currently placed at the top of our group. This team will be attending both Epic Lan 6 and Multiplays Insomnia 43 where we hope to make a big impression.
Our Benelux based Css team have just joined our organisation after we saw a lot of potential following their recent appearance at The Party 9 in Holland where they came a very respectable 3rd place knocking out 1st seed LowLanLions on the way. They also recently won the ESL Winter Cup.
This team will be attending Multiplays Insomnia 43 in August as well as any local Benelux lans that come up.
Our Nordic based Css team has recently just undergone a complete roster reshuffle after some unfortunate results meant things had to change. They will be competeing in the ESL Nordic Amateur series and we hope to see this new roster step up to the challenge.
Our Russian based Call of Duty4 team have gone from strength to strength over the last few months bringing in some very impressive results against some established and respected teams. Having just earned entry into the academy invite channels we hope they continue their progression and earn the rights to be in the pre.invite channel.
Recently they have been playing in the ESL where they have been playing extremely well and only missing out on a few finals due to the time difference between the EU and Russia. They have also been playing in the local leagues in Russia where they have been turning a lot of heads and making their way to finals and beating respected teams on the way. This team will be attending any local Lan events that come up in Russia over the coming months where we hope to achieve a top3 finish.

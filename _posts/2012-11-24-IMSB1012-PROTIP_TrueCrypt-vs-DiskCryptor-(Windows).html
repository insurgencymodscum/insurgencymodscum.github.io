TrueCrypt encrypted files can be accessed in Linux.

http://www.truecrypt.org/
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TrueCrypt 

DiskCryptor is better for encrypting disks. DiskCryptor encrypted disks cannot be accessed in Linux.

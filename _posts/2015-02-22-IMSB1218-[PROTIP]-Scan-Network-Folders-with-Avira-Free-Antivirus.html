--- layout: post title: "[PROTIP] Scan Network Folders with Avira Free Anti Virus - Use Symbolic Links (Windows)" date: '2015-02-22T23:04:00.000Z' author: insurgencymodscum2 tags: - Insurgency Mod Scum - OT-Noted - OT-PROTIP modified_time: '2015-04-14T23:04:55.987+01:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7256856399250838035.post-9000887597445735969 blogger_orig_url: http://insurgencymod.blogspot.com/2015/02/protip-scan-network-folders-with-avira.html --- Free version of Avira can not scan network folders (paid version does not have this limitation).

Fix: use symbolic links.

C:\>mklink /d "c:\folder" "\\NETWORK NAME\folder"

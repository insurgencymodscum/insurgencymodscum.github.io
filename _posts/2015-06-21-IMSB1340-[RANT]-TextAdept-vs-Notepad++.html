The following are some of my problems with TextAdept.

Always show tab bar.
Highlight current tab.
Awkward naming.

The above are not really "problems", but here's one: CAPS LOCK. Notepad++,  happily ignores CAPS LOCK, TextAdept on the other hand, refuses to do anything if your CAPS LOCK is keyed.

Maybe this is some design decision, but it is infuriating.

TextAdept is programmed by *one* guy, "Mitchell" (it was not easy finding this guy's full name BTW), and apart from the design choices  and the CAPS LOCK issue INSMODSCUM hasn't experienced any bugs. Notepad++ on the other hand seems to be programmed by the same guys who programmed Adobe Reader and Flash.

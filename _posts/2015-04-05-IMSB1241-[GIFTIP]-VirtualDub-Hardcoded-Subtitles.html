Download "xy-VSFilter_3.0.0.306_x86.zip".
Copy "VSFilter.dll" to "plugins32".
Rename to "VSFilter.dll.vdf".

In VirtualDub:
Video->Filters->Add Filter->TextSub.
Select "srt" file.

6.0 (289). Nice little low-budget, but surprisingly good movie if you have nothing else to watch. If you like movies with twists at the end (M. Night S. comes to mind), you would probably like this. Charlie from "Lost" and the nerd from "Chuck" are in this. And also a girl who looks like a guy (third from the left).

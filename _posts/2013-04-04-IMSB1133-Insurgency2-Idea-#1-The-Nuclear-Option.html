If a player caps all the objectives, and kills the entire opposing team, then...

If Insurgent: player gets a dirty nuclear bomb and detonates it, that will turn the entire team into zombies that are only able to be killed with a headshot. Security Forces get bolt-action rifles.

If Security Forces: player calls in a nuclear strike. Every player than sees the player who called in the nuke riding atop the bomb into destruction just like in Dr Strangelove. "AllTalk" is on and everybody else is muted, so the "hero" can say whatever he wants as he is riding down.

P.S. On hardcore mode, Security Forces players either get one clip, or one bolt-action rifle. Insurgent zombies get RPGs.

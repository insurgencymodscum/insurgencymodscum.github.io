Jack Black is usually found in mindless Hollywood drivel... but not this time. If you like Black and / or want to see him in a more serious role, give  Bernie (2011) [7.1 (2, 248)] a try. The movie is based on true events, and there are interviews with real (?) people throughout the movie (or possibly real interviews portrayed by actors).
